DS Solutions USA Chiro Premier King Mattress

Achieve the perfect balance of support and comfort with the DS Solutions USA Chiro Premier King Mattress. Designed for those who prioritize restful sleep and long-term investment, this mattress combines durability and premium materials to provide an unmatched sleeping experience.

Why Choose This Mattress?

Available with a gently firm comfort level, this mattress is ideal for maintaining proper spinal alignment, offering support without sacrificing comfort. The high-density polyurethane foam is used on both sides, ensuring a plush and resilient feel that adapts to your preferences.

Featuring a flippable design, the Chiro Premier King Mattress allows you to easily switch between comfort levels, extending its lifespan and giving you more flexibility. With 490 innerspring coils and 13 gauge main wire along with 6 gauge border wire, the mattress provides a durable and responsive support system.

The quilted fabric cover adds an extra layer of softness and protection, making it a great choice for people with allergies or who want to keep their mattress clean. As a made in USA mattress, it stands out for its quality craftsmanship and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Perfect for Every Need

This mattress is designed to fit seamlessly with any standard bed frame or box spring, making it a versatile and reliable addition to your bedroom. Whether you're looking for a king mattress protector, a flippable mattress cover, or an orthopedic mattress support, the Chiro Premier King Mattress is built to deliver on all fronts.

Whether you're an individual or part of a family, this mattress offers a comfortable and supportive sleep solution. Its long-lasting design ensures that you get the most out of your investment in both comfort and health.
