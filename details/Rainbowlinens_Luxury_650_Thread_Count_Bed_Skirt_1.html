Rainbowlinens Luxury Bed Skirt

Elevate your bedroom's style with our elegant and functional bed skirt. Made from 100% Egyptian cotton, it combines softness and durability for long-lasting comfort. The 650 thread count ensures a premium feel that enhances the look and feel of your bedding setup.

This tailored pleated design features two box pleats at the corners and three center pleats, adding a refined appearance to your bed. Its three-side coverage makes installation effortless, eliminating the need for a full four-sided skirt while keeping your space sleek and organized.

As a dust ruffle, it hides the gap between your mattress and bed frame, keeping your room tidy and dust-free. The soft cotton blend is gentle on floors and complements any bedroom decor, offering both practicality and a touch of sophistication. Whether you're looking for luxury or simplicity, this bed skirt delivers the perfect blend of both.
